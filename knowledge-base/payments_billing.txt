# BILLING KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Overview
Urban Threads accepts multiple payment methods and provides detailed billing information for all transactions. This guide covers payment processing, invoices, subscriptions, and billing inquiries.

## Accepted Payment Methods

### Credit & Debit Cards
**Supported:**
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards with Visa/MC logo
- Prepaid cards (must be Registered)

**Processing:**
- Charged when order ships
- Authorization hold placed at checkout
- Hold released if order cancelled
- Secure encrypted processing

### Digital Wallets
- **PayPal:** Instant processing
- **Apple Pay:** iOS devices only
- **Google Pay:** Android and web
- **Shop Pay:** Fast checkout for returning customers

### Other Methods
- **Amazon Pay:** Link Amazon account
- **Afterpay/Klarna:** Buy now, pay later (4 installments)
- **Cryptocurrency:** Bitcoin, Ethereum (beta)
- **Bank Transfer:** For orders over $1000
- **Purchase Orders:** Business accounts only

### Gift Cards & Store Credit
- Can be combined with other payment methods
- No expiration date
- Balance never expires
- Check balance online anytime

## Payment Processing

### When Charged
- **Pre-orders:** When item ships, not at checkout
- **Standard orders:** Within 24 hours of order
- **Subscription:** On renewal date automatically
- **Partial shipments:** Charged as items ship

### Authorization vs. Charge
- **Authorization:** Verifies funds available
- **Charge:** Actual transaction processed
- **Hold period:** Up to 7 days if order delayed
- **Release:** Automatic if order cancelled

### Payment Declined

**Common Reasons:**
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect card details
- Billing address mismatch
- Bank fraud protection
- Expired card
- Daily limit exceeded

**Solutions:**
1. Verify card details entered correctly
2. Check billing address matches bank records
3. Contact your bank to authorize transaction
4. Try alternative payment method
5. Contact support if persists

### VAT/Tax Invoices (International)
- VAT number included if provided
- Tax breakdown by jurisdiction
- Customs declaration value

## Subscriptions & Recurring Billing

### Subscription Types
**Premium Membership:**

## Taxes & Fees

### Sales Tax
- Calculated based on shipping address
- Varies by state/country
- Shown at checkout before payment
- Compliant with state internet sales tax laws
- Tax exempt? Upload certificate to account

### International Fees
**Duties & Import Taxes:**
- Customer responsibility
- Varies by country and item value
- Not included in checkout price
- Separate from Urban Threads payment
- Customs calculator available online

**Currency Conversion:**
- Automatic at current exchange rate
- May include small foreign transaction fee from your bank
- Rate locked at time of order
- Billed in your local currency

### Shipping Fees
- Calculated by weight and destination
- Free on orders $50+ (standard shipping)
- Expedited shipping: Additional cost
- International: Starts at $19.99

## Payment Security

### Security Measures
- **PCI DSS Compliant:** Highest security standard
- **SSL Encryption:** All transactions encrypted
- **Tokenization:** Card numbers never stored fully
- **3D Secure:** Additional verification for cards
- **Fraud Detection:** AI-powered monitoring

### What We Store
- **Encrypted:** Last 4 digits of card
- **Billing address:** For tax calculation
- **Email:** Order confirmation
- **NOT stored:** Full card number, CVV

### Unauthorized Charges
**If you see fraudulent charge:**
1. Contact your bank immediately
2. Report to support@urbanthreads.com
3. Change account password
4. Review account order history
5. File dispute with payment provider

## Refunds & Credits

### Refund Timeline
- **Processed:** 1-2 business days after return received
- **Bank posting:** 5-7 additional business days
- **Total time:** Usually 7-10 business days
- **Store credit:** Immediate upon processing

### Partial Refunds
- Returned some items from order
- Restocking fee applied
- Return shipping deducted
- Adjustment for promotion that no longer applies

### Failed Refunds
**Reasons:**
- Card expired or closed
- Account closed
- Bank rejected deposit

**Resolution:**
- Store credit issued automatically
- Contact us to update payment info
- Reissue refund to valid method

## Billing Disputes

### Disputing a Charge
**Process:**
1. Contact us first: billing@urbanthreads.com
2. Include order number and specifics
3. We investigate within 3 business days
4. Resolution or explanation provided
5. If unresolved, file with credit card company

### Common Disputes
- Duplicate charges
- Wrong amount charged
- Charged for cancelled order
- Not received but charged
- Quality issue not resolved

### Chargeback Process
- Last resort after working with us
- File with your credit card company
- Provide documentation
- Can take 60-90 days resolution
- Account may be restricted during investigation

## Common Questions

Q: Why was I charged twice?
A: Likely an authorization hold + actual charge. Hold will drop in 3-7 days.

Q: Can I split payment between two cards?
A: Not directly, but can use gift card + credit card combination.

Q: When will my refund arrive?
A: 7-10 business days from when we process the return.

Q: Can I get an invoice before paying?
A: Business accounts yes, personal accounts invoiced after order ships.

Q: How do I update my payment method for subscription?
A: Account > Subscriptions > Update Payment Method.

Q: What if my payment method expires?
A: Update in account settings. We'll email reminder before subscription renewal.

Q: Can I pay with multiple gift cards?
A: Yes, apply up to 5 gift cards per order.

Q: Are there foreign transaction fees?
A: Not from us, but your bank may charge their own fee.
