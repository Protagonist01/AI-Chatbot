# RETURNS & EXCHANGES KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Overview
Our returns and exchanges policy is designed to ensure you're completely satisfied with your Urban Threads purchase. We accept returns within 30 days and offer free exchanges.

## Return Policy

### Return Window
- **Standard items:** 30 days from delivery date
- **Sale items:** 15 days from delivery date
- **Final sale items:** No returns accepted
- **Gift purchases:** 60 days with gift receipt

### Return Conditions
Items must be:
- Unworn and unwashed
- Tags still attached
- Original packaging if applicable
- No signs of wear, alterations, or damage
- Accompanied by proof of purchase

### Non-Returnable Items
- Underwear and intimate apparel (hygiene reasons)
- Swimwear with removed hygiene stickers
- Pierced jewelry
- Custom or personalized items
- Digital gift cards
- Items marked "Final Sale"

## Exchange Policy

### **Free Exchanges**
We offer free exchanges for:
- Different size
- Different color
- Different style (same price or higher)

### Exchange Process
1. Log into account and request exchange
2. Select new item (size/color/style)
3. Original item shipped back (free return label)
4. New item ships immediately (don't wait for return)
5. If return isn't received within 14 days, original purchase amount charged

### Exchange Conditions
- Original item must meet return conditions
- New item available in stock
- Price difference handled as separate transaction
- One free exchange per order

## How to Return Items

### Online Returns
1. Visit urbanthreads.com/returns
2. Log in to your account
3. Select order and item(s) to return
4. Choose return reason
5. Print prepaid return label
6. Pack items securely
7. Drop off at carrier location
8. Track return shipment

### In-Store Returns
- Bring item(s) and receipt/order confirmation
- Any Urban Threads retail location
- Instant refund to original payment method
- Exchange items available immediately
- No shipping fees

### Return Without Receipt
- Store credit issued at lowest sale price (last 90 days)
- ID required for tracking purposes
- Limited to 3 returns per year
- Subject to verification

## Return Shipping

### Prepaid Labels
- Free return shipping on defective/wrong items
- $6.99 deducted from refund for standard returns
- Premium members: Free returns always
- Label valid for 45 days

### Return Packaging
- Original packaging preferred
- Alternative sturdy box acceptable
- Items must be protected during shipping
- Include return authorization slip
- Remove any old shipping labels

### International Returns
- Customer pays return shipping
- Contact support for return address
- Customs forms required
- Original shipping not refundable
- Refund minus international shipping fees

## Return Processing

### Timeline
1. **Return shipped:** Tracking number active
2. **Return received:** 1-3 days after carrier delivers
3. **Inspection:** 1-2 business days
4. **Refund processed:** 1 business day after approval
5. **Funds available:** 3-7 days depending on payment method

### Refund Methods
- **Credit/Debit card:** 5-7 business days
- **PayPal:** 2-4 business days
- **Store credit:** Immediate upon processing
- **Original payment:** Always refunded to source

### Partial Returns
- Returning some items from an order: Prorated refund
- Free shipping threshold: May charge original shipping if under $50
- Bundled discounts: Individual item price used
- Gift with purchase: Must return promotional item

## Quality Issues

### Defective Items
**Covered defects:**
- Manufacturing flaws
- Stitching coming apart (within 30 days)
- Color bleeding/fading (first wash)
- Zippers/buttons defective

**Process:**
1. Email photos to quality@urbanthreads.com
2. Include order number and description
3. Prepaid return label sent within 24 hours
4. Choose replacement or refund
5. Expedited processing (2-3 days total)

### Damaged During Shipping
- Report within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of packaging and damage
- Keep all packaging materials
- Full refund + shipping costs covered
- Replacement sent immediately

### Wrong Item Received
- Full refund including shipping
- Prepaid return label provided
- Correct item shipped at no charge
- Expedited shipping on replacement
- Keep wrong item if value under $15

## Exchange Details

### Size Exchanges
- Choose different size same item
- Free return shipping
- New size ships immediately
- If size unavailable, full refund option
- Size guides available on product pages

### Color Exchanges
- Any color available in same style
- Subject to availability
- Price same or lower: No additional charge
- Price higher: Pay difference
- Free return shipping included

### Style Exchanges
**Same price or lower:**
- No additional charges
- Free return shipping

**Higher price:**
- Pay price difference
- Charged when new item ships
- Free return shipping

**Lower price:**
- Price difference refunded
- Or store credit issued

## Restocking Fees

### When Applied
- Returns without tags: 15% fee
- Worn or washed items: 25% fee
- Missing packaging: $5 fee
- Custom orders: 30% fee (if returnable)

### Fee Waiver
No restocking fees for:
- Defective items
- Wrong item received
- Delayed Shipping (7+ days late)
- Premium member returns
- During promotional periods

- Never expires
- Stackable with promotions
- Transferable to others

### How to Choose

## Common Issues

### Return Not Received
**If tracking shows delivered:**
- Allow 3 business days for processing
- Check return was sent to correct address
- Contact support with tracking number

**If tracking stuck:**
- Wait 5 business days
- Contact carrier
- File claim if package lost
- Refund processed upon investigation completion

### Partial Refund Received
**Reasons might include:**
- Return shipping fee deducted
- Restocking fee applied
-Item arrived damaged/worn
- Missing components/tags
- Check email for explanation

### Exchange Out of Stock
**Options:**
- Wait for restock (notify when available)
- Choose alternative item
- Full refund processed
- Store credit with 10% bonus

- Prepaid recycling label provided

### Sustainable Packaging
- Return in eco-friendly packaging
- Reuse original packaging

- Recycling instructions included

## Common Questions

Q: Can I return sale items?
A: Yes, within 15 days of delivery.

Q: What if I lost the return label?
A: Generate a new one through your account or contact support.

Q: Can I exchange a gift?
A: Yes, with or without gift receipt for store credit or exchange.

Q: How long until I get my refund?
A: 7-10 business days after we receive your return.

Q: Can I return items bought with store credit?
A: Yes, refund returned as store credit.

Q: What if my return is rejected?
A: We'll email reason why and return item to you at no charge.

Q: Can I return custom items?
A: Generally no, but contact support for special circumstances.
