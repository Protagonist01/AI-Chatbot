# ORDERS & TRACKING KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Overview
Track your Urban Threads orders from purchase to delivery. Get real-time updates, modify orders, and resolve shipping issues.

## Order Status

### Order Stages

**1. Order Placed**
- Confirmation email sent immediately
- Order number assigned
- Payment processed
- Estimated delivery date provided

**2. Processing**
- Items being prepared for shipment
- Quality check performed
- Packaging in progress
- Typically 1-2 business days

**3. Shipped**
- Package handed to carrier
- Tracking number active
- Shipping confirmation email sent
- Estimated delivery updated

**4. In Transit**
- Package moving through carrier network
- Real-time tracking updates
- Location updates every 12-24 hours
- Delivery date confirmed

**5. Out for Delivery**
- Package on delivery vehicle
- Arriving today
- Delivery window provided (if available)
- Final tracking update

**6. Delivered**
- Package delivered successfully
- Delivery confirmation email
- Signature/photo proof (if applicable)
- Delivery location noted

### Checking Order Status

**Online:**
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click on order number
4. View detailed status and tracking

**Without Account:**
- Use order number + email
- Visit urbanthreads.com/track
- Enter order details
- View status and tracking

**Via Email:**
- Check confirmation email
- Click "Track Order" link
- Redirects to tracking page

## Order Modifications

### Before Shipment

**Can be modified:**
- Shipping address
- Shipping speed
- Add items to order
- Apply discount code
- Update payment method

**How to modify:**
1. Contact support immediately
2. Provide order number
3. Specify changes needed
4. Confirm updated total (if applicable)
5. Receive confirmation email

**Modification deadline:**
- Standard orders: Before processing (usually 2-4 hours)
- Express/Overnight: Within 30 minutes of order

### After Shipment

**Limited modifications:**
- Cannot change address directly
- Can request carrier reroute (fee may apply)
- Cannot add items
- Cannot change shipping speed

**Reroute options:**
- Contact carrier directly with tracking number
- UPS My Choice: Free address change
- FedEx Delivery Manager: Reroute options
- USPS: Intercept service ($15-20)

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Delivered

**If tracking shows delivered but not received:**
1. Wait 24 hours (may be scanned early)
2. Check all possible delivery locations
3. Ask neighbors and household members
4. Check mailroom or building office
5. Look for delivery photo in tracking
6. Contact carrier after 24 hours
7. File claim with us after 48 hours

**Common hiding spots:**
- Behind planters or furniture
- Side or back door
- Garage
- With neighbor
- Building mail room
- Parcel locker

### Delayed Delivery

**Common delay reasons:**
- Weather conditions
- High package volume (holidays)
- Carrier delays
- Incorrect address
- Customs clearance (international)
- Delivery exceptions

**What to do:**
- Check tracking for updates
- Allow 2 extra days for standard shipping
- Contact carrier for details
- Contact us if delay exceeds 5 days

**Compensation:**
- Express/Overnight late: Full shipping refund
- Standard late: Case-by-case basis
- Must file claim within 7 days

### Damaged Package

**Upon delivery:**
1. Accept package but note damage on receipt
2. Take photos of box and contents
3. Keep all packaging materials
4. Report within 24 hours
5. Email photos to claims@urbanthreads.com

**Do not:**
- Refuse package (complicates claim)
- Discard packaging
- Use damaged items

**Resolution:**
- Replacement shipped immediately
- Full refund option
- Return label provided
- Claim processed within 3-5 days

### Lost Package

**If tracking stuck or no updates:**
- Standard: Allow 2 extra days
- Express: Contact after 1 day delay
- International: Allow 5 extra days
- File claim after waiting period

**Claim process:**
1. Contact us with order number
2. Provide tracking details
3. Investigation initiated
4. Resolution within 7-10 days
5. Replacement or refund issued

### Stolen Package (Porch Piracy)

**Steps to take:**
1. File police report
2. Contact us within 48 hours
3. Provide police report number
4. Submit claim form
5. We'll work with you on resolution

**Prevention:**
- Require signature
- Use alternative pickup location
- Install security camera
- Request hold for pickup
- Ship to work address

## International Orders

### Customs & Duties

**Customer responsibility:**
- All customs fees and import taxes
- Varies by country and item value
- Typically 0-30% of declared value
- Plus VAT/GST where applicable

**Customs delays:**
- Can add 3-10 days to delivery
- Random inspections common
- Provide accurate customs info
- Respond to customs requests promptly

**Detained packages:**
- Contact local customs office
- Provide required documentation
- Pay any assessed fees
- Refused delivery: Return shipping at customer expense

### International Tracking

**Tracking limitations:**
- May have gaps during customs
- Updates less frequent
- Final delivery tracking varies by country
- Use local postal service for final mile

### Supported Countries
We ship to 150+ countries including:
- Canada, Mexico (5-7 days)
- UK, Europe (7-14 days)
- Australia, New Zealand (10-16 days)
- Asia (8-21 days)
- South America (10-21 days)

## Common Questions

Q: Why hasn't my order shipped yet?
A: Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. Check order status for updates.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
A: Yes, if order hasn't shipped yet. Contact us ASAP.

Q: What if I won't be home for delivery?
A: Add delivery instructions or use carrier hold options.

Q: Do you ship to military addresses (APO/FPO)?
A: Yes! Use standard shipping, typically 10-15 business days.

Q: Can I track multiple orders at once?
A: Yes, log into account and view all orders with tracking.

Q: What if my package is stuck in customs?
A: Contact your local customs office with tracking number.

Q: How do I know when my order will arrive?
A: Check tracking for estimated delivery date, updated as package moves.

Q: Can I pick up my order instead of shipping?
A: Currently no in-store pickup, but carrier pickup points available.
