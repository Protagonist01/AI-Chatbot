## How to Measure

### Women's Measurements

**Bust:**
- Wrap tape measure around fullest part of bust
- Keep tape parallel to floor
- Wear non-padded bra for accuracy

**Waist:**
- Measure around natural waistline
- Location: above belly button, below rib cage
- Don't suck in - breathe normally

**Hip:**
- Stand with feet together
- Measure around fullest part of hip and bottom
- Approximately 8 inches below waist

**Inseam:**
- Measure from crotch to desired pant length
- Use well-fitting pants as reference
- Or measure from crotch to ankle

### Men's Measurements

**Chest:**
- Measure around fullest part of chest
- Under arms, across shoulder blades
- Keep tape comfortably snug

**Waist:**
- Measure around natural waistline
- Location: where you wear belt
- Don't hold breath

**Sleeve:**
- Start at center back of neck
- Over shoulder point
- Down to wrist with arm bent

**Neck:**
- Measure around base of neck
- Allow two fingers of space
- Important for collar fit

### Shoe Measurements

**Foot Length:**
1. Stand on blank paper
2. Mark longest toe and heel
3. Measure distance between marks
4. Measure both feet (use larger)
5. Add 0.5" for comfort
6. Compare to size chart

**Foot Width:**
- Measure widest part of foot
- Standard width included in sizing
- Wide widths: Add W suffix (e.g., 9W)

## Fit Guides by Category

### Tops & Shirts

**Regular Fit:**
- Comfortable, not tight
- Room in chest and waist
- Suitable for layering
- Most versatile option

**Slim Fit:**
- Follows body contours
- Fitted through chest and waist
- Modern, tailored look
- Size up if between sizes

**Relaxed Fit:**
- Generous through body
- Extra room  for comfort
- Casual, loose silhouette
- Great for loungewear

### Dresses

**Bodycon:**
- Very fitted, stretchy fabric
- Hugs curves
- Size up for comfort
- Check fabric content (spandex %)

**A-Line:**
- Fitted at top, flares from waist
- Forgiving at hips
- Classic, flattering cut
- True to size

**Shift:**
- Loose, straight cut
- Minimal shaping
- Comfortable fit
- Size down for more structure

### Pants & Jeans

**Skinny:**
- Tight from hip to ankle
- Stretch fabric recommended
- Size up if between sizes
- Check rise (low/mid/high)

**Straight:**
- Same width hip to ankle
- Classic fit
- True to size
- Versatile styling

**Relaxed/Boyfriend:**
- Loose through hip and thigh
- May taper at ankle
- Size down for less volume
- Casual vibe

### Outerwear

**Fitted:**
- Should fit over one light layer
- Close to body
- Sleeves to wrist when arms down
- Zip/button without pulling

**Regular:**
- Room for 1-2 layers underneath
- Comfortable movement
- Sleeves cover wrist
- Most common choice

**Oversized:**
- Intentionally large
- Room for multiple layers
- Dropped shoulders
- Fashion-forward look

## Fit Recommendations

### Between Sizes?

**Size up if:**
- You prefer looser fit
- Busty or broad shoulders
- Item is fitted style
- Fabric has little stretch
- Planning to layer

**Size down if:**
- You prefer fitted look
- Item is oversized style
- Fabric has significant stretch
- Between measurements lean smaller

### Fabric Matters

**No Stretch (Cotton, Linen):**
- Order true to size or up
- Minimal give
- Check actual measurements
- May shrink if washed hot

**Some Stretch (Cotton/Spandex Blend):**
- True to size works well
- 2-5% spandex: slight give
- Comfortable all-day wear

**High Stretch (Jersey, Performance Fabric):**
- Can size down if preferred
- 10%+ spandex: significant stretch
- Molds to body
- Great for fitted styles

## Size-Specific Notes

### Shoes
**Half sizes:**
- Round up for sneakers
- Round down for heels
- True for boots

**Width options:**
- Standard (B for women, D for men)
- Wide (D for women, 2E for men)
- Extra Wide (2E for women, 4E for men)

### Accessories

**Belts:**
- Order 2 sizes larger than waist pants size
- Eg: Size 32 pants = Size 34 belt
- Adjustable holes for flexibility

**Hats:**
- Measure head circumference
- One size fits most: 21.5"-23"
- Adjustable straps on many styles

## Common Questions

Q: Are your sizes consistent across all items?
A: Generally yes, but fit varies by style. Check product-specific fit notes.

Q: Do clothes shrink after washing?
A: Cotton may shrink 3-5%. Follow care instructions. Pre-shrunk items marked.

Q: How do I know if an item runs small/large?
A: Customer reviews often mention fit. Look for "True to size" comments.

Q: Can I see how items fit on different body types?
A: Yes! Check customer photos in reviews section.

Q: What if measurements fall in different size ranges?
A: Go with largest measurement and tailor down if needed.

Q: Do you offer custom sizing?
A: Not currently, but we offer alterations service for purchased items ($15-45).
