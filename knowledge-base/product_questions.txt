## Product Care & Maintenance

### General Care Tips
**Washing:**
- Turn inside out before washing
- Wash similar colors together
- Use cold water for darks
- Gentle cycle for delicates
- Avoid overloading machine

**Drying:**
- Air dry when possible
- Low heat for tumble dry
- Remove promptly to prevent wrinkles
- Hang knits to maintain shape

**Storage:**
- Fold heavy knits (don't hang)
- Use padded hangers for structured items
- Store shoes with shoe trees
- Keep leather goods in dust bags

### Specific Material Care
**Leather:**
- Condition every 3-6 months
- Use leather cleaner for stains
- Avoid water exposure
- Professional cleaning for major issues

**Wool:**
- Hand wash or dry clean
- Use wool-specific detergent
- Lay flat to dry
- Store with cedar or lavender

**Silk:**
- Hand wash in cold water
- Use gentle detergent
- Never wring or twist
- Iron on low with pressing cloth
