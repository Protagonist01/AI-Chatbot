# HUMAN AGENT ESCALATION KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Overview
While our AI assistant handles most requests efficiently, some situations require the expertise and empathy of a human agent. This guide explains when and how to connect with our customer support team.

## When to Escalate to a Human Agent

### Automatic Escalation Triggers
The AI will automatically connect you to a human agent if:
- Complex account issues (multiple failed attempts)
- Sensitive personal information needed
- Legal or compliance matters
- Fraud or security concerns
- Complaint about service quality
- Emotional distress detected
- Technical issues the AI cannot resolve
- Custom requests requiring approval

### You Can Manually Request a Human Anytime
Simply say:
- "I want to speak to a human"
- "Connect me to an agent"
- "This isn't helping, I need real support"
- "Let me talk to a person"
- "Escalate to human"
- Or select option 7 from the category menu

## What to Expect

### Connection Process
1. **Request acknowledged:** "Connecting you to the next available agent..."
2. **Wait time provided:** Typical wait: 30 seconds to 5 minutes
3. **Queue position shown:** "You are #3 in queue"
4. **Agent introduction:** Name and employee ID provided
5. **Context transfer:** Agent sees your conversation history

### Agent Availability
**24/7 Support:**
- Critical issues (account locked, fraud)
- Order tracking
- General questions

**Business Hours (9AM-9PM EST):**
- Returns and exchanges
- Technical support
- Billing disputes
- Account modifications

**Specialized Agents:**
- Monday-Friday only
- Business/corporate accounts
- Legal inquiries
- Media/partnership requests

### Average Response Times
- **Peak hours (12PM-4PM EST):** 3-5 minute wait
- **Off-peak:** 30 seconds - 2 minutes
- **Overnight (12AM-6AM EST):** 1-3 minutes
- **Holidays:** 5-10 minute wait

### Priority Queue
**Skip the line if:**
- Premium/VIP member
- Order value $500+
- Prior escalation unresolved
- System outage affecting you
- Time-sensitive matter (same-day shipping)

## Agent Capabilities

### What Human Agents Can Do

**Immediate Actions:**
- Override system restrictions
- Issue instant refunds/credits
- Expedite shipping at no charge
- Cancel orders after cutoff time
- Waive fees and charges
- Access detailed order history
- Modify orders before shipping
- Create custom solutions

**Approvals & Exceptions:**
- Return items past 30-day window
- Accept returns without receipt
- Price adjustments beyond policy
- Bulk order special pricing
- Custom product requests
- Account limit increases
- Payment plan arrangements

**Complex Resolutions:**
- Multi-order issues
- Account merging
- Historical data requests
- Legal document preparation
- Executive escalation
- Partnership inquiries
- Media relations

### What Agents Cannot Do
- Change core company policies
- Offer unauthorized discounts over 30%
- Access other users' accounts
- Modify credit card charges directly (must go through processor)
- Recover deleted account data (permanent after 30 days)
- Override legal/compliance requirements

## Preparing for Human Contact

### Information to Have Ready
- **Order number** (if applicable)
- **Account email address**
- **Detailed description** of issue
- **Steps already taken** to resolve
- **Desired outcome**
- **Supporting documentation** (photos, receipts)
- **Timeline** of events

### Screenshots & Photos
For visual issues:
- Product defects
- Website errors
- Packaging damage
- Sizing comparisons
- Receipt/invoice
- Tracking information

### Making the Most of Your Call

**Do:**
- Be clear and concise
- Stay calm and polite
- Have information ready
- Write down agent name/ID
- Note reference numbers
- Ask for email summary

**Don't:**
- Be abusive or threatening
- Withhold relevant information
- Hang up without reference number
- Refuse reasonable solutions
- Demand impossible outcomes

## After Speaking with an Agent

### Follow-Up
- **Confirmation email:** Sent within 1 hour
- **Reference number:** Save for future contact
- **Action items:** Review what agent will do
- **Timeline:** Note when to expect resolution
- **Feedback survey:** Optional, helps improve service

### If Issue Not Resolved
1. Contact again with reference number
2. Request escalation to supervisor
3. Ask for confirmation of resolution plan
4. Set specific timeline expectations
5. Request follow-up call/email

### Escalation Path
If still unsatisfied:
1. Request supervisor callback
2. Email complaints@urbanthreads.com
3. Submit formal complaint via website
4. Request executive escalation
5. External review (BBB, Consumer Protection)

## Common Questions

Q: How long will I wait for an agent?
A: Typically 30 seconds to 5 minutes depending on time and queue.

Q: Can I request a specific agent?
A: Not for initial contact, but can request same agent for follow-up.

Q: Will the agent see my chat history with the AI?
A: Yes, full context is transferred for continuity.

Q: Can I get a callback instead of waiting?
A: Yes! Provide phone number and we'll call within 30 minutes.

Q: What if the agent can't help me?
A: They'll escalate to a supervisor or specialist who can.

Q: Are agents in the US?
A: We have global support centers. Tier 2+ are US-based.

Q: Can I email the agent directly?
A: Individual agent emails provided for ongoing issues only.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the agent's response?
A: Ask for supervisor or use reference number to escalate.

## Tips for Best Experience

### Best Times to Contact
- **Least busy:** Early morning (6-9AM EST), late evening (8-11PM EST)

5. Note reference numbers

### For Complex Issues
- Email first with detailed information
- Attach relevant documents
- Request phone callback

- Faster resolution on call
