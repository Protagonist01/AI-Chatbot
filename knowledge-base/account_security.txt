# ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT KNOWLEDGE BASE

## Overview
Your Urban Threads account gives you access to order history, saved payment methods, wishlists, and exclusive member benefits. This guide covers everything related to account management.

## Creating an Account

### Registration Process
1. Visit urbanthreads.com/register
2. Enter email address
3. Create secure password (8+ characters)
4. Agree to terms of service
5. Verify email (check inbox/spam)
6. Complete profile (optional)

### Account Benefits
- Faster checkout
- Order tracking
- Wishlist and saved items
- Exclusive member deals
- Birthday rewards
- Referral bonuses
- Address book

### Guest Checkout
- No account required
- Limited order tracking
- No saved preferences
- Can create account later using order email

## Login Issues

### Forgotten Password

**Reset process:**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter registered email address
3. Check email for reset link (expires in 24 hours)
4. Create new password
5. Log in with new credentials

**Didn't receive email?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address spelling
- Wait 10 minutes and try again
- Contact support if still not received

### Account Locked
**Reasons:**
- Too many failed login attempts (5 within 30 min)
- Suspicious activity detected
- Payment dispute in progress
- Terms of service violation

**Unlock:**
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
- Click "Unlock Account" link in security email
- Contact support for immediate assistance
- Verify identity with order history

### Email Not Recognized
**Possible reasons:**
- Account created with different email
- Never created account (used guest checkout)
- Typo in email address
- Account deleted/suspended

**Solutions:**
- Try alternative email addresses
- Search email for "Urban Threads" confirmations
- Create new account
- Contact support with order numbers

## Account Security

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- Special character recommended
- Cannot be previous 5 passwords
- Change every 90 days (recommended)

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
**Setup:**
1. Account Settings > Security
2. Enable 2FA
3. Choose method: SMS or Authenticator app
4. Verify phone number or scan QR code
5. Save backup codes securely

**Benefits:**
- Extra layer of security
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Required for withdrawals and sensitive changes
- Insurance against account takeover

### Security Best Practices
- Never share password
- Use unique password (not used elsewhere)
- Enable 2FA
- Log out on shared devices
- Review account activity regularly
- Set up login alerts

## Deleting Your Account

### Before You Delete
- Download order history
- Use remaining store credit
- Cancel active subscriptions
- Process any pending returns

### Deletion Process
1. Account Settings > Privacy
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Confirm reason for deletion
4. Re-enter password
5. Permanent deletion in 30 days

### Data Retention
- Order history: 7 years (tax compliance)
- Personal info: Deleted after 30 days
- Marketing data: Immediately removed
- Payment data: Removed immediately

### Reactivation
- Within 30 days: Contact support
- After 30 days: Create new account
- Order history not recoverable

## Common Questions

Q: Can I change my registered email?
A: Yes, in Account Settings. Verification required.

Q: How do I cancel Premium membership?
A: Account > Subscriptions > Cancel Membership.

Q: What happens to my credits if I delete my account?
A: They're forfeited. Use them first!

Q: Can someone else use my account?
A: No, accounts are non-transferable.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: Email, phone, or live chat - all linked in account dashboard.

Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes, we use bank-level encryption and never store full card numbers.

Q: Can I have multiple accounts?
A: Not recommended, but not prohibited. One account per person preferred.

Q: How do I unsubscribe from emails?
A: Click unsubscribe at bottom of any email, or manage in account settings.
