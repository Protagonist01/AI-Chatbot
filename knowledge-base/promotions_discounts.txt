## Discount Codes

### How to Use Discount Codes

**Applying codes:**
1. Add items to cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Find "Promo Code" or "Discount Code" field
4. Enter code exactly as shown
5. Click "Apply"
6. Discount reflects in order total

**Code format:**
- Usually all caps: SAVE20, FREESHIP
- Case-sensitive
- No spaces
- One code per order (unless stated otherwise)

### Code Not Working?

**Common issues:**
- **Expired:** Check expiration date
- **Minimum not met:** Order total below requirement
- **Excluded items:** Some products not eligible
- **Already used:** One-time use codes
- **Typo:** Enter exactly as shown
- **Stacking not allowed:** Can't combine with other offers

**Solutions:**
1. Verify code spelling and capitalization
2. Check terms and conditions
3. Remove excluded items
4. Add more items to meet minimum
5. Contact support if still not working

### Where to Find Codes

**Official sources:**
- Email newsletter (subscribe for exclusive codes)
- Homepage and promotion banners
- Social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter)
- Mobile app notifications
- Seasonal sale announcements
- Abandoned cart emails

**Beware of:**
- Expired codes on third-party sites
- Fake or invalid codes
- Codes for different retailers
- Always verify on our official channels

## Price Matching

### Price Match Policy
**We'll match:**
- Identical item (same size, color, style)
- Lower price from authorized retailer
- Within 14 days of purchase
- Proof of lower price required

**We won't match:**
- Third-party marketplace sellers
- Auction sites
- Closeout/liquidation sales
- Different product versions
- Membership-only prices

### How to Request
1. Email pricematch@urbanthreads.com
2. Include order number
3. Provide link to lower price
4. Screenshot of competitor price
5. Receive refund of difference

## Common Questions

Q: Can I use multiple discount codes?
A: No, only one promo code per order (unless specifically stated).

Q: Do discounts apply to sale items?
A: Depends on the promotion. Check terms for "excludes sale items."

Q: How do I get notified about sales?
A: Subscribe to emails, follow social media, or download the app.

Q: Can I apply a discount after placing an order?
A: Contact us within 1 hour of ordering, we may be able to help.

Q: Do rewards points expire?
A: No, Urban Threads rewards points never expire.

Q: Can I combine student discount with sale prices?
A: No, student discount applies to regular-priced items only.

Q: How often do you have sales?
A: Weekly deals, monthly sales, and major seasonal events.

Q: Can I price match a sale from last month?
A: No, price matching applies to current competitor prices only.
